SIM Activation
SIM卡插入手機並收到網絡訊號，即已自動激活SIM卡並啟用服務。
SIM卡服務之有效期由激活及啟用服務當日起計。成功啟用後，你的手機將會收到確認短訊，短訊內容包括SIM卡的服務號碼及有效日期等資訊。若未能透過上述方法成功啟用，請照以下聯絡當手機設定：
  • 開啟數據漫遊功能
  • 流動網絡 > 接入點名稱 (APN) 設定為 3gnet
  • 開啟VoLTE功能
  • 重新啟動手機
Insert the SIM card into the mobile phone and the mobile network signal will be detected. This means the SIM card has been activated automatically and the service has been commenced. The validity period of the SIM card service starts from the day of activation and service commencement. Upon successful activation, your mobile phone will receive a confirmation SMS with the SIM card service number and expiry date etc. If you are unable to activate the SIM card, please check your mobile phone setting as below.
  • Turn on data roaming
  • Mobile Data > APN : 3gnet
  • Turn on VoLTE
  • Restart your mobile phone
